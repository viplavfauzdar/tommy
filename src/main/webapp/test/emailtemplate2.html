F.A.Q.

Frequently Asked Questions about Finance Georgia and Investments

What is Finance Georgia?

Finance Georgia is a Curated Equity Crowdfunding platform.

What is Equity Crowd Funding?

Equity Crowd Funding is a new way for start-ups to raise money and a new way for investors including the general public to invest in them. Instead of getting prizes, trinkets or rewards you get actual shares in the company.

What does Curated mean?

What this means is that not only does the Finance Georgia team help vet (select), the best companies to offer investors; but they additionally go through a selection process by a leadership council, the FGLC.

What is the FGLC?

The FGLC stands for: the Finance Georgia Leadership Council. The council is comprised of select experts from a variety of professions. From venture capitalists, to professors, physicians, musicians and athletes, we have the right mix of people helping review the companies that want to get on our platform.

Can I only Invest in New Companies?

We offer you the best new companies, projects, real estate deals and even charities Georgia has to offer. Our expertise allows us to be deal agnostic.

Can anyone Invest of only the very wealthy?

For the first time the general public also has the opportunity to invest in such a manner. Our minimum investment is $500, so now the opportunity to invest is open to all.
